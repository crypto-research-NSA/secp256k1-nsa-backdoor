
FAQ
Q: Have you broken Bitcoin?
A: No. We found proof of parameter manipulation, not a key extraction method.

Q: Is Bitcoin unsafe?
A: The backdoor exists but requires ~2^80 ops to exploit. Only state-level actors could feasibly attack.

Q: Why would NSA do this?
A: Strategic backdoor - maintain ability to break encryption if needed.

Q: Can this be fixed?
A: Yes - migrate to transparently generated curves like Curve25519.

Q: Did Satoshi know?
A: Probably not. secp256k1 was a pre-existing SEC standard.
